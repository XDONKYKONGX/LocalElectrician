Our Story

From humble beginnings to Devon's trusted electrical service provider

A Family Legacy of Electrical Excellence

Founded in 1993 by John Smith in Kingsteignton, Local Electrician began as a one-man operation serving our local community. What started as a small business with a single van has grown into Devon's most trusted electrical service, now spanning three generations of the Smith family.

Our commitment to quality workmanship and personal service has remained unchanged through the years. We've wired homes across Devon through economic booms and recessions, always adapting to new technologies while maintaining our core values of integrity and reliability.

Today, we're proud to serve both longtime customers who remember our early days and new clients who appreciate our modern approach to electrical solutions. The tools and technologies have changed, but our dedication to treating every home like it's our own has never wavered.
